Biography

Ezekiel "Zeke" Thomas Oien, born on June 12, 1998, leads a life marked by adventure, resourcefulness, and a relentless pursuit of his dreams. Zeke, a native of Vancouver, WA, has also called Portland, OR, and Seattle, WA his home, embracing the vibrant cultures and scenic beauty of the Pacific Northwest.

An interesting and unique aspect of Zeke's life is his living situation. Zeke, along with his wife Krystal, has chosen a non-traditional path by converting a school bus into a mobile tiny home. This unconventional choice reflects their adventurous spirit and commitment to a lifestyle defined by simplicity and mobility.

Zeke's love for travel has taken him to various destinations, but Mazatlan, Mexico, holds a special place in his heart as his favorite travel locale. His journeys are often accompanied by his three beloved cats, Rainier, Hood, and Bear, who add joy and companionship to his life.

Professionally, Zeke has demonstrated versatility and a knack for learning new skills. His career journey began at G6 Trampoline Park and later found him mastering the intricate art of locksmithing at Seattle Pacific University. It's here that he recalls his funniest job memory - getting locked out of a bathroom with only a small towel, a testament to his ability to find humor in life's unexpected moments.

Zeke is a man of diverse interests, ranging from outdoor activities like skiing and hiking to engaging in strategic board games. He's a person who sets clear goals for himself, aspiring to become a homeowner, start his own business, and cultivate his own food.

Among his personal achievements, Zeke takes pride in having reconstructed a bus into a cozy abode, self-taught locksmith skills, and holding track and cross-country records at Multnomah University. These accomplishments speak volumes about his determination, creativity, and athleticism.

Zeke's journey, from transforming a bus into a home to excelling in various endeavors, paints the picture of a man who embraces life's challenges with a smile and an unwavering spirit of adventure.
